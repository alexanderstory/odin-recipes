Mint Chicken

Description

To prepare mint chicken, start by marinating boneless chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, minced garlic, ginger paste, lemon juice, salt, and a generous amount of freshly chopped mint leaves. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes to infuse the flavors. Meanwhile, prepare a spice blend by combining ground cumin, coriander, turmeric, and a pinch of cayenne pepper for a hint of heat.

Once the chicken has marinated, heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a bit of oil. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until they are golden brown on all sides and cooked through. Garnish the mint chicken with additional freshly chopped mint leaves before serving. This fragrant and flavorful dish pairs well with rice, naan bread, or a crisp green salad for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Prepare the spice blend
  3. Cook the chicken
  4. Add the spice blend
  5. Finish with mint
  6. Serve
